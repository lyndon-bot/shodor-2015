Blog: Oct. 4th 2014

Today I learned about office ethics. Most of it I already knew so the class was a breeze. I enjoyed the experience and I look forward to learning more. After todays lessons I truly believe that I have made the right decision in deciding to join Shodor. Ron gave a very enthusiastic presentation on office ethics. The determination that Ron showed had all the students and even me looking of in pure awe and captivation. He creatively demonstrated to us the rules and guidelines of Shodor. After Ron gave a stunning performance Dr.Pannoff gave a lecture on the basic properties of the scientific method and the fundamentals of computer program as well as system modeling. The presentations of both of the excellent orators publicized the skill and durability of the Shodor staff. I have concluded that I wish to be part of this wonderful team and further my development.
